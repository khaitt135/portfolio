About

My name is Khai Tran. I am a junior at Northeastern University studying Business Analytics and Data Science, drawn to the space where performance marketing meets data engineering. I approach my work with a beginner's mindset and a bias toward building things that last.

Outside of academics, I find balance in the things that keep me grounded. Drawing lets me be creative without constraint. Photography teaches me to be fully present in a moment. Snowboarding and running are how I build mental resilience: one steep slope and one long mile at a time. And writing, I have come to realize, is how I engage with my own thinking from a different angle.

This portfolio is a reflection of all of those things: curiosity, presence, and a commitment to growth. Let's join me.
