Reflection & Learnings
üîÅ
Prompting is a system, not a shot
The biggest insight was treating every AI interaction as part of a repeatable system ‚Äî defined inputs, structured outputs, human validation, and feedback loops. One-shot prompting is a starting point, not a methodology.
üß†
Structure beats creativity in analytical prompting
Instruction prompting, role framing, and format control consistently outperformed open-ended prompts for data analysis. The model performs best when given a clear job description, not a vague request.
üë§
Human gates are the quality multiplier
The review steps at 3.1 and 5.0 were not bureaucratic checkpoints ‚Äî they were the highest-leverage moments. Every correction fed back as training material improved every subsequent prompt in the chain.

Ethics isn't a constraint ‚Äî it's a design principle
Applying AHES to each prompt forced a higher standard of output. Prompts that passed ethical review consistently produced more nuanced, defensible, and actionable insights than those that skipped it.
